Unicorn

Rarity meter(1 being most rare and 5 being common): 2

Basic information

Unicorns are quite rare creatures but are very kind and loyal pets. However they can cause mischief with all the magic contained within them.

shelter

Your unicorn will be happiest living and sleeping inside a stables or barn, where he or she is sheltered from the wind and the rain. Your unicorn’s stall should include some nice fresh hay or straw, or wood shavings.Also remember to clean the stable regularly to make sure your unicorn is happy and healthy.

dietary needs

Unicorns love mush—and some even like cinnamon raisin oatmeal! They love sugar-beet biscuits as treats, as well as carrots and apples. Unicorns also require a constant supply of fresh water
